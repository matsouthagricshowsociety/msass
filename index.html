    Services

    The Matebeleland South Agricultural Show Society plays a pivotal role of tantamount importance in the markerting, exposing and connecting the local farm to the extensive business world of buyers and investors, promoting the Matebeleland-South farming industry.

    Showcasing

    This is the display, parading and showcasing of the agricultural produce in the Province, exposing their outputs to the nation at large.

    Markerting

    Connecting even the remote and secluded Matebeleland-South farmer who is deep in the growth point to the markert and potential buyers and investors.

    Grading

    Carrying out an intense competition between farm produce including field produce, animal husbandry, selecting and awarding the best efforts and grades.

    Networking

    Creating a strong network between farmers and producers to share and compare notes for the continual growth of their individual projects.

    Growth

    All this leading to the inevitable growth of the Agricultural Industry in the Province by giving them the support and recognition they so much deserve.
